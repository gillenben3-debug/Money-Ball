## 1. Introduction

Money Ball ("we," "our," or "us") is a personal finance application that helps users track spending, manage debt, and build healthy financial habits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Money Ball mobile application.

By using Money Ball, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, and password when you register
- **Financial account credentials:** When you connect a bank account, you authenticate directly with your financial institution through Plaid. We do not store your bank login credentials.

### 2.2 Information From Third-Party Services (Plaid)
When you link a financial account, we receive the following data from Plaid on your behalf:
- Account names, types, and balances
- Transaction history (descriptions, amounts, dates, categories)
- Institution names

We use Plaid to access this data. Plaid's privacy policy is available at [https://plaid.com/legal/#privacy-policy](https://plaid.com/legal/#privacy-policy).

### 2.3 Information Generated by the App
- **Usage data:** Features used, screens viewed, in-app actions
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, app version
- **AI Advisor interactions:** Messages you send to the AI Advisor feature

### 2.4 Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect Social Security numbers, full payment card numbers, or government-issued ID numbers.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide, operate, and improve the Money Ball application
- Display your financial data, spending breakdowns, and net worth
- Power the AI Advisor feature to give personalized financial insights
- Send you important account and service notifications
- Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Plaid | Bank account linking and data retrieval | Authentication tokens |
| Anthropic (Claude API) | AI Advisor responses | Anonymized financial context you choose to share |
| Cloud infrastructure provider | App hosting and data storage | Encrypted user data |
| Law enforcement / legal process | Legal compliance | As required by law |

We require all third-party service providers to maintain appropriate security standards and to use your data only as directed by us.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Your Data Is Stored
Your data is stored on encrypted cloud servers. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is protected with TLS 1.2 or higher encryption. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.

### 5.2 Security Practices
We implement the following security measures:
- Multi-factor authentication on all systems that access your data
- Role-based access controls limiting who can access production data
- Regular security reviews and dependency audits
- API keys and credentials stored securely, never in source code

### 5.3 No Guarantee
While we implement strong security practices, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account information | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| Financial transaction data | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| AI Advisor conversations | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| Usage/analytics data | 12 months rolling |

When you delete your account, your personal and financial data is permanently deleted from our production systems within **30 days**. Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being purged.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Data portability** — Request your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** — Disconnect linked bank accounts at any time through the app
- **Opt out** — Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **gillen.ben3@gmail.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Consumer Consent

Before connecting a bank account through Plaid, you will be presented with a clear disclosure explaining:
- What data will be accessed
- How it will be used
- That Plaid acts as an intermediary

Your explicit consent is required before any financial data is retrieved. You can revoke this consent at any time by disconnecting your accounts within the app.
